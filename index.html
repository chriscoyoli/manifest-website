Consign with Manifest

Your closet's next chapter starts online

No more paper forms. Tell us about your pieces from home, sign your agreement once, and your drop-off appointment takes minutes instead of paperwork.

1

Start online

Fill in your details and list up to 20 items. First time? You'll review and e-sign the consignment agreement right from your phone.

2

Book your drop-off

Pick a time that works. Bring items clean, pressed, and on hangers. We review everything with you in person.

3

Track and get paid

Watch your items sell anytime in your Consignor Connect portal. Take payout as a check or use your balance in store.

Est. 2024 in Carmel Valley

Letting go makes room for what you love

Manifest began with a simple idea: the dress that no longer brings you joy might be exactly what brings someone else theirs. We're a concept consignment store celebrating local artisans, sustainable choices, and the stories women share over the racks.

Every consignment keeps clothing in circulation and out of the landfill, supports a neighbor, and funds the community events that make this more than a store.
